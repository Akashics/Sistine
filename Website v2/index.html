What is Sistine?

Sistine was created back whenever when Kashall wanted to listen to weeb music whilist annoying his friends. He doesn't do that anymore, but still likes to share his music, thus Sistine the Anime Bot was born (with more features too!).

Sistine currently features a number of things not limited to High Quality Music, Auto & Self Role-ing, Cute Animal Images, Image Generator Commands, Chuck Norris & Dad Jokes, Weird Memes, and Personal & Guild Profiles and Leaderboards! There is always content being added, so join our community and listen our update announcements!
